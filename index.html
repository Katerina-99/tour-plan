Emma Whitson

Stayed 9 Des, 2019

Great staff, very helpful and friendly receptionists. The hotel is small but very cozy. There is a parking lot (you need to enter the gate under the arch). Breakfast: it is convenient to bring it to your room. But the breakfast is very monotonous: an egg, a slice of apple, yogurt, a packet of juice (not tasty), a sandwich and a muffin. I would like a little more variety, after 2 days this breakfast is very boring. They bring breakfast until 10 am, this time was very suitable for us. Great location. To the metro 10 minutes along a busy street. There is everything you need nearby. The room itself is clean, everything worked properly, there are no questions about the number. The only thing is that if the windows face the road, it will be noisy. For people with sensitive sleep, I would recommend rooms on the opposite side of the road.
